# BMW

Control and view your BMW

## Get Started

To get started you will need to provide your Connected Drive username and password, as well as selecting your region.

## Available Commands

- Honk your horn
- Flash the lights
- Enable climate control
- Lock your doors
- Unlock your doors
- View tire pressuer
- View your car
- View next services

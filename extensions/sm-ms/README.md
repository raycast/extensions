# SM.MS

SM.MS image hosting service to upload and share images.

**⚠️Warning:**
The size of uploaded images is limited to less than 5M.

**How to get API secret token?**

- Register an account on [SM.MS](https://sm.ms),
- After entering the home page, click on **User** in the upper right corner and select **Dashboard**,
- Click on the **API Token** in the left sidebar,
- Click on the blue **Generate Secret Token** button,
- Copy the generated _Secret Token_, back to this extension and fill in the **API secret token** form.
